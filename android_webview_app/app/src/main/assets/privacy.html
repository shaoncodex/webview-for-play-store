Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the WebView App Privacy Policy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information in connection with your use of our application.

Information Collection

The WebView App is designed to operate offline and does not collect personal information. All content is stored locally on your device within the application's assets folder.

Information We Do Not Collect:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Browsing history outside of the app
  • Device information

Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: This permission is only used for potential future content updates and is not used to collect or transmit personal information.
  • Network State: This permission allows the app to determine if an internet connection is available for potential content updates.

Data Storage

All content in the WebView App is stored locally on your device within the application's assets folder. This content is included with the application itself and is not dynamically downloaded or updated without your knowledge.

Third-Party Services

The WebView App does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs that would collect or process your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
